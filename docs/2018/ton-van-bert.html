Developing after 50: how to avoid becoming a manager - Ton van Bart

13:30 - 14:30 lightning talk

On Quora and other fora I sometimes see questions popping up where people ask if it is possible to be a developer after 30. In this talk I will try to answer this question (spoiler: yes you can), which things to do and not do, and choices I made for myself to remain technical.
