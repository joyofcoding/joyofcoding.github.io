Speaker instructions

Practical information for Joy of Coding 2017 speakers.

Please see the attendee joining instructions for more information.

Arrival in Rotterdam

The six main conference speakers are staying five minutes’ walk from the venue.

Hotel address: Rotterdam Marriott Hotel, Weena 686, 3012CN Rotterdam

Speakers’ dinner

The six main conference speakers can meet each other and the organisers before the pre-party for dinner at a Thai restaurant, 17:30-19:00. We’ll arrange taxis from the hotel.

Address: De Kleine Ondeugd, Oostzeedijk 348, Rotterdam, next door to the pre-party venue.

Presentations

Each conference session is 40 minutes long.

The projector has 16:9 format and an HDMI connector. Let us know if you need an adaptor.

Filming

InfoQ will be filming the presentations to put online. Please sign the video release form at the registration desk, when you collect your name badge.

Emergency contacts

The emergency number in the Netherlands is 112. In case of minor emergencies, contact the organisers: Martin (+31 681 488635), Cies (+31 646 469087) or Felienne (+31 647 216980).

Lightning talks

Send us your slides in PDF format in advance. We’ll combine these into a single presentation, which we’ll display on our laptop.

Lightning talks will be strictly limited to five minutes, with a visible timer.

Lightning talk speakers will have a handheld microphone and a clicker.
